Origami Swan

Start with a square of origami paper

Step 1: Fold in half along the diagonal and unfold.

Step 2: Fold one side up to meet the crease you have just made and repeat on the other side.

Step 3: Turn the model over and fold one side up to meet the crease you have just made and repeat on the other side as well.

Step 4: Mountain Fold the whole model in half

Step 5: Fold the neck up by using an outside reverse fold.

Last Step: Fold the head down. Use an outside reverse fold again.

Now You Have A Origami Swan!

步骤1:沿对角线对折并展开。

第 2 步：将一侧向上折叠以符合刚刚制作的折痕，然后在另一侧重复此操作。

步骤 3:将模型翻过来, 将一侧向上折叠以符合刚刚制作的折痕, 并在另一侧重复此操作。

第4步:山形将整个模型对折

第5步:使用外侧反向折叠将颈部向上折叠。

最后一步：将头向下折叠。再次使用外侧反向折叠。

现在你有了一只折纸天鹅！

Origami In Schools

Origami started in the 17th century in Japan. By the mid-1900s, it had become a popular art form worldwide.

First Book

The world's oldest book on origami, published in 1797, is titled Hiden Senbazuru Orikata (How to Fold a Thousand Cranes)
